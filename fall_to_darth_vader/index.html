Anakin Skywalker: The Fall to Darth Vader

A comic book journey from hero to villain

From Hero to Villain

Anakin Skywalker, once the "Chosen One", begins his journey as a Jedi Knight. But his struggles with the Jedi Council and inner conflicts start to cloud his future.

The Dark Temptation

Feeling powerless to save his loved ones, Anakin is drawn into the temptations of the Dark Side, with Emperor Palpatine offering him a way to gain ultimate power.

The Duel with Obi-Wan

In a heartbreaking fight on Mustafar, Anakin's former mentor, Obi-Wan Kenobi, must battle him to stop his transformation into Darth Vader.

The Birth of Darth Vader

Severely wounded after the duel, Anakin is reborn as Darth Vader, encased in black armor and ready to serve the Emperor as the enforcer of the Galactic Empire.
